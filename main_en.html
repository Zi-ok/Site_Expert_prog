UA EN

The direct conversion program allows you to obtain quantitative estimates (weights) of expert qualitative indicators for the object under study. The method used in the program does not require experts to conduct any preliminary quantitative assessments of these indicators. It is sufficient to rank the qualitative indicators (the number of indicators is not limited thanks to the developed transitive structure of indicator placement) and, if possible, add complex relations such as: “The weight of the first indicator is greater than that of the third and fourth combined”; “The weight of the fifth indicator is equal to the sum of the weights of the seventh and eighth,” etc. After that, using the simplex method, calculate the weights of the expert qualitative indicators. The program provides the ability to adjust the obtained weights.

keywords: conversion of qualitative indicators, ranking of qualitative indicators, quantitative assessment of weights, complex relations, weight adjustment.
