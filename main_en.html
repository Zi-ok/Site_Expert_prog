The proposed method used in the conversion program includes ranking qualitative indicators (without limiting the number of indicators), determining the quantitative values of the weights of each indicator, and, if necessary, adjusting the weights obtained. In addition to simple ranking relations, the program provides for the introduction of complex relations, such as: “The weight of the first indicator is greater than that of the third and fourth combined”; “The weight of the fifth indicator is equal to the sum of the weights of the seventh and eighth,” etc. The described method does not require experts to perform any quantitative assessments of these indicators, which significantly improves their work and increases the reliability of their conclusions.

keywords: conversion of qualitative indicators, ranking of qualitative indicators, quantitative assessment of weights, complex relations, weight adjustment.
