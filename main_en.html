A PROGRAM TO CONVERT EXPERT QUALITATIVE CHARACTERISTICS OF OBJECTS INTO QUANTITATIVE ESTIMATES

UA EN

The proposed method used in the transformation program includes ranking qualitative indicators (without limiting the number of indicators), determining the numerical value of the weight of each indicator and, if necessary, adjusting the obtained weights. In addition to simple ranking relationships, the program provides for the introduction of complex relationships such as: “The importance of the first indicator is greater than the third, fourth, taken together”; “The weight of the fifth indicator is equal to the sum of the weights of the seventh and eighth”, etc. The described method does not require experts to provide any numerical characteristics of these indicators and thus significantly improves their work and increases the reliability of the conclusions of the latter.
